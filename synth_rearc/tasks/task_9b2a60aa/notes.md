The summary hints identified the right high-level ingredients, but I corrected the placement rule from the official examples.

- The copied shapes are arranged parallel to the marker line, not perpendicular to it.
- Marker gaps do not place copies directly on the marker coordinates. Instead, the gap between consecutive copied 3x3 boxes is `marker_gap - 1`.
- The red marker that matches the template color sits at one end of the ordered marker sequence, and the original red template is the corresponding end copy.
