`arc2_opus46_summary.json` was used as a starting hint, but it needed a precise correction.

The crucial rule is not just that a dot extends toward the nearest path. Each singleton lies on the straight continuation of exactly one endpoint of a thin orthogonal path, the empty bridge to that dot is filled with the path's original color, and the same number of cells is recolored from the opposite endpoint with the dot color.

`arc2_sonnet45_summary.jsonl` was discarded because it stays at the vague "nearest structure" level and does not capture the endpoint-alignment constraint or the exact length-matching between the bridge and the recolored prefix.
