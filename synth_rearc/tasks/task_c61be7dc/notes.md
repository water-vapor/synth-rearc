The output bar length is exactly the total number of input `5` cells, and the two full `0` separator lines are always redrawn immediately adjacent to that centered bar.

`arc2_opus46_summary.json` was closer on the orientation and line-redrawing pattern, but it still omitted the count-preserving bar length. The implemented verifier and generator therefore use the official examples' stronger rule: detect whether the paired full separators are vertical or horizontal, compute the center of mass and total count of the `5` cells, then redraw the `5`s as a centered straight bar of that exact length with adjacent full `0` lines on either side.
