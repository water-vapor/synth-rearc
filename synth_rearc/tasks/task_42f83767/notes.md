`arc2_opus46_summary.json` was accurate enough to use as the starting hypothesis: the task is an ordered color-chip palette plus an ordered `5`-glyph library, expanded over the lower-left color matrix.

`arc2_sonnet45_summary.jsonl` described the same three-part layout but was too vague about the exact mapping. The official examples show that the top-left chips determine the left-to-right order of the glyph templates, and each lower matrix cell is replaced by the corresponding square glyph recolored from `5` to that cell's color.
