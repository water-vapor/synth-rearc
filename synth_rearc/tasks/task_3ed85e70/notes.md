Legend-based completion task.

The large `3` backdrop contains fully specified multicolor motifs. Objects on the `0`
background are partial reductions of those motifs: inner cores, dominant-color filled
versions, or exact half/quadrant crops. The output completes each matching fragment
into the corresponding legend motif while leaving unrelated objects unchanged.

The generator keeps the same structure:
- one large `3` panel on a grid edge
- 2-3 complete motifs embedded in the panel
- several scattered fragments outside the panel
- optional unrelated distractors
